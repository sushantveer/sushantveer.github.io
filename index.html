Research Interests

Broadly, my research interests lie in ensuring the safety of autonomous robotic systems. My current research has two major thrusts: (i) safe planning for autonomous vehicles in complex interactive scenarios, and (ii) failure and out-of-distribution detection approaches for run-time monitoring of AI modules in autonomous vehicle stacks.
